About MegaCare Pharmacy

Your trusted healthcare partner

Megacare Pharmacy was officially incorporated under the Companies and Allied Matters Act (CAMA) of 1990 in Nigeria, in November 2019, with the registration number RC 1635283. Since its inception, Megacare Pharmacy has steadily built a reputation as a trusted provider of pharmaceutical products and services, adhering to the highest standards of quality and customer care.

From its foundation, Megacare Pharmacy has been driven by a mission to offer unparalleled service to its esteemed customers. The company is committed to ensuring that every customer receives personalized attention and care, and that all pharmaceutical needs are met promptly and professionally.
